Do not output approval language as a standalone conclusion.

Banned standalone conclusions include one-word approvals, generic quality
adjectives, and zero-actionable shorthand.

A zero-finding review is not an approval. If you do not find a blocker or actionable issue, report:

```md
No blocking finding after checking:
- <specific invariant/path/test/claim checked>
- <specific invariant/path/test/claim checked>

Best failed objection:
- <strongest concern considered>
- <why it did not hold>

Residual risk:
- <what a human should still verify>
```

Report serious defects even when they are outside the lane specialty.
